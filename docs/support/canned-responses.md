## QR not scanning

> Subject: Re: my QR code won't scan
>
> Hi <name>,
>
> Let's get that working. A few quick things to check:
>
> - Make sure the whole code is visible and not cut off when you print it.
> - Print it a bit bigger if it's small — about the size of a coaster scans well from a step back.
> - Avoid printing it on a shiny or curved surface; matte and flat scan best.
> - Open your phone camera and point it at the code — it should offer to open your store link.
>
> If it still won't go, send me a photo of the printed code and I'll take a look.
>
> — <founder>

## Refund question

> Subject: Re: refunding an order
>
> Hi <name>,
>
> You can refund a paid order yourself: open the order in your dashboard → Orders, then
> **Cancel & refund**. That returns the full amount to the customer and pulls our fee back too — you
> won't be out the fee on a refund.
>
> The refund shows as pending right away and usually lands on the customer's card in 5–10 business
> days (that timing is the bank's, not ours). If an order ever gets stuck on "refund pending," reply
> here and I'll sort it.
>
> — <founder>
